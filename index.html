Composer

Adrian is a British internationally-recognised orchestral and chamber music composer. His Violin Concerto recently received its world premiere to a three-minute standing ovation at London’s Southbank Centre, played by soloist Fenella Humphreys and the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra. He's received commissions from many areas of the classical music world including the BBC Proms, and has worked with some of the most distinguished orchestras in the world, including the RPO (the recent concert was their fifth collaboration) and BBC Symphony Orchestra amongst many others.

Growing up immersed in the world of large scale orchestral music both as a player in South Africa’s leading youth string orchestra and surround by music at home, orchestral music is in his DNA. Following formal training at Goldsmiths University in London, his musical style is accessible to all, with performers deriving as much pleasure playing as do audiences being swept away by his expansive harmonies and strong melodies. He’s a strong advocate of live music being performed for the enjoyment of both the performers and the audience; a unique moment to be shared and remembered.

Orchestral & choral works

Adrian's orchestral works include a Violin Concerto, recently premiered to wide acclaim at London's Southbank, with Fenella Humphreys and the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra.

A Fist Full Of Fives (2016) was performed by the BBC Symphony Orchestra on Radio 3.

War Horse: The Story in Concert, which had its premiere at the Royal Albert Hall in 2016 and featured Michael Morpurgo and Joanna Lumley reading the book on-stage, accompanied by the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra and singers.

War Horse Suite, a 25-minute symphonic orchestral work derived from the score for the show premiered in 2010 by the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, that along with a new BBC Proms commission Some See Us, featured as the centrepiece of a *War Horse Prom* in the 2014 Proms season.

Chamber works

Adrian's chamber music has been performed regularly by various groups including the concert violinist Fenella Humphreys, the 6-piano group Piano Circus, Blaze Ensemble, Ensemble Perpetuo and others.

Theatre

Adrian is one of the UK's most widely-acclaimed theatre composers, having written and produced the scores for War Horse, The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time, Coram Boy, Angels in America and many others.

Adrian's work on War Horse has received international acclaim in multiple productions worldwide including the USA, Canada, Australia, Berlin, Paris, the Netherlands and China. Adrian received an Olivier Award in 2013 for his work on The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time

He received a Tony nomination for his score for Angels in America in 2018

His most recently-completed projects are the scores for Dr. Semmelweis (Bristol Old Vic, dir. Tom Morris) and Murder on the Orient Express (Chichester Festival Theatre, dir. Jonathan Church)
